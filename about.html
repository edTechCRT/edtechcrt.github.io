edTechCRT

About

edTechCRT is a non-commercial support group and information hub for education technology professionals created in response to the challenges we are all facing during the current COVID19 pandemic. Our goals are (1) to set up an ad hoc communication network, and (2) to create a virtual to collect and share critical information.

We invite anyone in schools or who work in education technology to join the Slack channel and Confluence wiki set up for this project.

Note: This community will consist of education and business professionals. No solicitation is allowed.

edTechCRT is an initiative of The Proof Group and its education partners. Proof is a virtual technology team with a deep history of working with schools to improve education operations through technology strategy and software systems.
